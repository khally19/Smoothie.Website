Mango Pinneaple Smoothie

You will need:

  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • 1/2 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1 cup frozen diced pineapple
  • 1 cup frozen diced mango
Directions: In a blender, combine the orange juice, yogurt, pineapple, and mango. Blend until completely smooth. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
